### What was built today

**Phase N48 — Frozen days on heatmap** (~30 lines added):
Streak-freeze days now render as a distinct ice-blue cell in the N8 heatmap SVG, with a legend entry below the grid. Fixes known gap #6.

- **IDB read**: `renderHeatmap` now loads `streakFrozenDates` from settings at the top (via `getSetting`), builds a `Set` for O(1) per-cell lookup.
- **Ice-blue cells**: for any day that is (a) not in the future, (b) has zero reading activity, and (c) is in the frozen set, the rect fill is `#7dd3fc` (Tailwind sky-300). Days that have both reading activity AND a freeze token show as the normal green-level colour (activity takes precedence).
- **Tooltip**: frozen cells show `"YYYY-MM-DD: streak frozen 🧊"` on hover instead of the plain date.
- **Legend**: a `.hm-legend` row is appended below the SVG if any frozen date falls within the visible 53-week window. Shows a small ice-blue dot + "streak frozen" label. Hidden when no frozen dates exist in the window.
- **CSS (Phase N48 block)**: 3 new rules — `.hm-legend`, `.hm-legend-item`, `.hm-legend-dot`.
