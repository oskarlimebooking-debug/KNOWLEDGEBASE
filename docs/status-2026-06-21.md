## Known gaps / follow-ups

1. **N20 — Notes panel re-renders on each tab open**: no lazy caching.
2. **N12 — Live test on Vercel** pending.
3. **Mobile long-press assign** — right-click doesn't fire on touch.
4. **N22 — minutes goal**: only counts time in chapter view.
5. **N38 — No highlight created**: quick card creates SRS card from selection but NOT an annotation highlight. Intentional but could be surprising.
6. **N45 — book group order not sorted**: book group headers appear in `Object.entries` order; could sort groups by title for consistency.
7. **N46 — progress bar not refreshed on unmark**: the book progress bar still shows old completion % until the user navigates back to the book view.
8. **N47 — history collapsed state resets**: history collapses every time `renderDeckMgr` is re-called (search/filter). Could persist toggle state in a `let _histOpen = false` variable.
9. **N47 — no tag filter in history**: session history shows all sessions; there's no way to see sessions for a specific tag filter.
10. **N48 — legend hidden when all frozen dates are outside the 53-week window**: edge case; frozen dates >1 year old are silently absent. Acceptable.
