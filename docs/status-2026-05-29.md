## Feature details

### Trigger condition
- `goalType !== 'none'` AND `progress >= goalAmount` AND `confettiDate !== todayStr()`
- Fires at most once per calendar day per device (IDB-persisted)
- Subsequent calls to `renderDailyAndStats` on the same day do nothing (guard check)

### Animation
- 18 particles launched radially from the goal chip center
- Each particle is a different hue: `#ff6b6b` (red), `#ffd93d` (yellow), `#6bcb77` (green), `#4d96ff` (blue), `#ff922b` (orange), `#cc5de8` (purple), cycling
- Particles rotate 540° (1.5 full turns) as they fly outward and fade to opacity 0
- Duration: 0.9s, ease-out; DOM nodes removed after 950ms (no leaks)

### IDB key
- `confettiDate` — stores `YYYY-MM-DD` string; read/written via existing `getSetting`/`setSetting`
