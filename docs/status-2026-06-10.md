## Feature details

### N38 — Highlight-to-card quick shortcut

- **Trigger**: Any text selection ≥ 2 chars in the chapter article fires the existing `_showAnnMenu()` flow
- **New button**: `→ Card` (`.ann-card-btn`) added alongside existing `+ Note` and `📖 Define` buttons
- **Card form** (`#ann-card-form`, `.ann-card-form`):
  - Hidden by default; shown with `.visible` class on button click
  - Front textarea: pre-filled with `selectedText.slice(0, 500)`, all selected on open
  - Back textarea: empty, keyboard focus stays on front until Tab
  - "Save card" button + Cancel button in `.ann-card-quick-row`
- **Save**: `_saveQuickCard()` — validates both fields non-empty, calls `srsAddCard(ctx.chapterId, ctx.bookId, front, back)`, shows "Card added ✓" or "Already in deck" toast, closes menu via `_hideAnnMenu()`
- **Keyboard**: Enter in Back (without Shift) saves; Tab moves front→back naturally
- **Max width**: `ann-menu` widened to 340px to accommodate two-column form
